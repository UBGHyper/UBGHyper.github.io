Welcome to UBGHyper

Discover the best free online games in one place!

Drive Mad

Drive Mad is a physics-based driving game where players control a quirky vehicle through increasingly difficult courses filled with obstacles, ramps, and jumps. With simple controls and unpredictable mechanics, it challenges players to navigate wild terrains while avoiding crashes while trying to reach the finish line.

Burrito Bison

Burrito Bison is an action-packed, quirky game where you play as a luchador bear, launching yourself through candy-filled landscapes to smash through enemies and collect upgrades. With fast-paced gameplay, epic combos, and hilarious visuals, you’ll battle your way through levels to defeat the evil gummies and escape the candy world.

Dont Escape 3

Don’t Escape 3 is a tense, point-and-click adventure where you must solve puzzles and make crucial decisions to survive an impending doom. Set in a ruined spaceship, you must carefully plan your actions to avoid disaster and escape the terrifying consequences of your choices.
